HeatExchangerGUI Matlab program

These files must all be in the same folder:
	- Counter.png
	- CounterFlowFunction.m
	- HeatExchangerGUI.fig
	- HeatExchangerGUI.m
	- Parallel.png
	- ParallelFlowFunction.m

Variables you can change: 
	- Flow direction (parallel or counter flow)
	- Mass flow rates of hot and cold fluids
	- Specific heats of hot and cold fluids
	- Hot and cold fluid inlet temperatures
	- Overall heat transfer coefficient
	- Heat exchanger length
	- Heat exchanger interface tube diameter
